Login

If you haven't already, head to the login section and join the action! Dont have an account yet? dont worry, creating an account is super easy, simply fill in ur name, email and password on the register page and your good to go!

Bid on listings

You have to be logged in to bid on listings. When you create an account you automatically get 1000 credits, spend them wisely! Simply scroll trough or search for the listing you want, click on it to view that specific listing and place ur bid! you can view prior or new bids listed on the listing specific page.

Create a listing

Create listings to grow your credit count! You have to be logged in to create a listing. Enter your profile page, and click the button "create a listing", fill out the form and send in ur new listing! Keep the description short and simple, add as many images as you want.
